About NotAnEmulator

NotAnEmulator (NAE) is a project for recreating and simulating OSes in JavaScript. it was made in 2023 by Gabriel Fernandez, the creator of UnInternet and AnarchoMedia the idea was taken from Visnalize's Win7 Simu. the goal is to recreate Windows, Apple and Linux OSes.
